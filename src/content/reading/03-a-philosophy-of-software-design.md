The central idea is that complexity is the enemy, and interfaces are one of the main places where complexity either gets hidden or leaks everywhere.

## Notes

- Prefer deep modules with simple interfaces.
- Treat special cases as a design smell.
- Write comments that explain intent instead of restating code.
