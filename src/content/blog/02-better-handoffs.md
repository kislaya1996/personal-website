A handoff is successful when the next person can make progress without reconstructing the entire decision trail from Slack, commits, and memory.

## The checklist

- State the goal in one sentence.
- List the important constraints and discarded options.
- Link the places where the actual work lives.
- Call out the sharp edges and unanswered questions.

The checklist should be short enough to use on an ordinary Tuesday. If it becomes a process artifact nobody wants to fill in, it has already failed.
